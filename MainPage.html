About The Curator:

A doctoral degree gold medalist in art history from University of Cambridge and have been working with Victoria and Albert Museum in London for the past 17 years.

Favourite Paintings:

Our Curator has very unique taste in painting with array of contemporary and abstract paintings.

During such unprecedented times when we have to practice social distancing, organizing a proper exhibition can be very challenging. But worry not, we bring to you the Blah Blah's virtual painting exhibition where you can view all the famous paintings from around the world without leaving the safety and comfort of your home.
We sincerely hope that you love this as much as the traditional exhibition and enjoy it as much as our team did in creating it.
